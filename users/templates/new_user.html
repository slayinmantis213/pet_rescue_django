Welcome to Creature Classes!

This is a game about rescuing and rehabilitating mystical creatures. Before you get started, here are a few things you should know:

Rescuing a creature requires 5 gifts. Don't worry, you get your first 10 gifts just for being willing to help! When you first bring them home the creature will be a little worse for wear, so you'll want to make sure you give them a name and Visit them.

While Visiting you can Play and Train with your creature: Playing nurtures and regenerates the creature and, if they're having a good time, they'll probably give you a gift! Training is intense and provides your creature with a lot of experience toward a level up, but it is taxing!

Your beloved creatures are, at their core, wild and free and not built for confinement. It is best if they some day move on from their rehabilitation. Fortunately it isn't all bitterness! When you decide a creature is ready to return to their natural habitat, Release them. In thanks for your help, the creature will reward you with gifts equivalent to their current level!

If you Release a creature at level 10, when they are fully and truly ready to return to nature, the creature will thank you with a feather : the highest and most coveted honor among creatures and their helpers.

After they're inactive 15 minutes creatures regain all of their energy but lose a little bit of happiness. Be warned! If a creature's happiness falls too low, they will leave, awarding none of their usual farewell appreciation.

You can revisit this page at any time by clicking the help link located to the right of your username on your dashboard.

Good Luck!
